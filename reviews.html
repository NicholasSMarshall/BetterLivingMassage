Is this a better option for relief than seeing a chiropractor?
Massage therapy can be a better option for relief if your discomfort is rooted in muscle tension, stress, or soft tissue pain. It provides a relaxing, hands-on approach that targets the muscles directly to ease tightness and promote circulation. While chiropractic care focuses more on joint and spinal alignment, massage offers a gentler experience that's ideal for ongoing stress relief and muscular issues.
