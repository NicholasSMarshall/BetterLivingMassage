Our Offerings
At Better Living Massage Therapy, we offer a variety of experiences, including Swedish massage, deep tissue, hot stone, AromaTouch therapy, prenatal massage, and more. See below for a complete list.
Our Events
Whether you're hosting a work conference, a party, or any event where relaxation is welcome, chair massages are a perfect way to restore energy and boost morale. For just $85 an hour, we bring professional chair massage services to your event for all your guests.
Swedish Massage
Swedish Massage offers a gentle, calming experience designed to help you unwind. Using long, flowing strokes, gentle kneading, and circular motions, the massage therapist works on the outermost layers of muscle to ease tension and promote relaxation. It’s an ideal option for first-time clients or those who prefer a softer touch, with sessions available at $60 for 60 minutes or $75 for 90 minutes.
Thirty Minute Massage
The 30 Minute Massage is a problem-focused treatment designed to address specific areas of pain or discomfort, such as headaches, neck tension, or localized muscle strain. The massage therapist concentrates on these targeted areas to provide effective relief, with each session priced at $40.
Custom Therapeutic
The Custom Therapeutic Massage is tailored to address your specific areas of tension and discomfort. Using a blend of techniques—such as deep tissue work, trigger point therapy, and myofascial release—the massage therapist targets muscle knots and areas of tightness to provide lasting relief. This personalized approach is available at $60 for 60 minutes or $75 for 90 minutes.
Prenatal Massage
Prenatal Massage is a specialized treatment designed for expectant mothers, addressing the unique physical and emotional changes that occur during pregnancy. Using specially designed cushions and a side-lying position to avoid pressure on the abdomen and lower back, the massage helps reduce anxiety, relieve muscle and joint pain, and improve circulation. Sessions are available at $70 for 60 minutes or $85 for 90 minutes.
doTerra AromaTouch
The doTerra AromaTouch Technique is a specialized massage that incorporates eight essential oils applied along the spine, neck, and feet to promote relaxation, reduce stress, and support overall well-being. This technique offers a calming, aromatic experience and is ideal for those seeking the therapeutic benefits of essential oils. The session is priced at $70 for 60 minutes.
Cupping
Cupping is a therapeutic add-on service that involves creating negative pressure inside a cup, which gently draws the skin upward and separates layers of superficial fascia. This technique helps bring old blood and cellular debris to the surface, encouraging fresh circulation in the area. The round marks left behind are often mistaken for bruises but are a natural and temporary result of the process.
Hot Stone Therapy
Hot Stone Massage is a therapeutic add-on service that uses smooth, heated basalt river rocks to help relax tense muscles, improve circulation, and restore soft tissue function. The stones are placed on specific areas of the body and may also be used during the massage to apply deeper, soothing pressure, enhancing the overall sense of relaxation and relief.
