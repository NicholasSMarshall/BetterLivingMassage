Our Services

Swedish Massage
Swedish Massage offers a gentle, calming experience designed to help you unwind. Using long, flowing strokes, gentle kneading, and circular motions, the massage therapist works on the outermost layers of muscle to ease tension and promote relaxation. It’s an ideal option for first-time clients or those who prefer a softer touch.
Thirty Minute Massage
The 30 Minute Massage is a problem-focused treatment designed to address specific areas of pain or discomfort, such as headaches, neck tension, or localized muscle strain. The massage therapist concentrates on these targeted areas to provide effective relief.
Custom Therapeutic
The Custom Therapeutic Massage is tailored to address your specific areas of tension and discomfort. Using a blend of techniques—such as deep tissue work, trigger point therapy, and myofascial release—the massage therapist targets muscle knots and areas of tightness to provide lasting relief.
Prenatal Massage
Prenatal Massage is a specialized treatment designed for expectant mothers, addressing the unique physical and emotional changes that occur during pregnancy. Using specially designed cushions and a side-lying position to avoid pressure on the abdomen and lower back, the massage helps reduce anxiety, relieve muscle and joint pain, and improve circulation.
Manual Lymphatic Drainage
Manual Lymphatic Drainage helps to ease lymphedema and edema and also can help with detoxing your body. This is a gentle technique that uses very light, rhythmic strokes to help move lymph through the body. Trained by a Vodder therapist and certified (through written and hands-on testing) in MLD of arms/chest and trunk/legs. Bandaging may be included if needed.
Add-ons
Hot stones, cupping, aromatherapy, and other add-ons may be used at no additional charge. See below for all add-ons offered.
doTerra AromaTouch
The doTerra AromaTouch Technique is a specialized massage that incorporates eight essential oils applied along the spine, neck, and feet to promote relaxation, reduce stress, and support overall well-being. This technique offers a calming, aromatic experience and is ideal for those seeking the therapeutic benefits of essential oils. The session is priced at $70 for 60 minutes.
Cupping
Cupping is a therapeutic add-on service that involves creating negative pressure inside a cup, which gently draws the skin upward and separates layers of superficial fascia. This technique helps bring old blood and cellular debris to the surface, encouraging fresh circulation in the area. The round marks left behind are often mistaken for bruises but are a natural and temporary result of the process.
Hot Stone Therapy
A hot stone massage is a distinctive type of massage that can help you relax and alleviate tense muscles, enhance circulation, and revitalize damaged soft tissues in your body. This form of massage requires the use of basalt River rocks, which are placed on various points of your body. Before being used, the rocks are heated and can be applied to target specific areas.
